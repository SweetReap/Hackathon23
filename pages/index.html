Graffiti N' NYC is a dynamic web app designed to guide art enthusiasts and curious explorers through a curated collection of galleries around the globe.

With the use of Google Maps API and OpenNYC APIs, users can effortlessly pinpoint, browse, and discover nearby art spaces, exhibitions, and events, enriching their cultural journey with every click. Whether you're seeking contemporary masterpieces or hidden gems, our intuitive interface ensures a seamless and immersive gallery-hopping experience tailored to your artistic preferences.
